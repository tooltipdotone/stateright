## Introduction

Placing an order on our platform is fast, easy, and secure. Whether you're buying for yourself or sending a gift, we ensure a smooth experience from start to finish.

## Steps to Place Your Order

1. **Browse Products**  
   Explore our latest collections by category or use the search bar to find exactly what you need.

2. **Add to Cart**  
   Select your item, customize if needed (size, flavor, etc.), and click **Add to Cart**.

3. **Review Your Cart**  
   Go to the cart icon at the top-right, double-check your items, and click **Proceed to Checkout**.

4. **Fill in Delivery Details**  
   Provide your shipping address and contact information. We deliver across the country!

5. **Choose Payment Method**  
   Pick from secure payment options including **bKash, Nagad, Card, or Cash on Delivery**.
