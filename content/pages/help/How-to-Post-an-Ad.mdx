# How to Post an Advertisement

Follow these simple steps to post your first ad on eClassify:

## Step 1: Create an Account

1. Click **Sign Up** in the top right corner
2. Fill in your email and create a password
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile information

## Step 2: Choose a Category

1. Click **Post Ad** from the main menu
2. Select the appropriate category for your item
3. Choose a subcategory if applicable

## Step 3: Add Product Details

### Basic Information
- **Title**: Write a clear, descriptive title
- **Description**: Provide detailed information about your item
- **Price**: Set a fair price for your item
- **Condition**: Select the condition (New, Used, etc.)

### Photos
- Upload high-quality photos (up to 10 images)
- Show different angles of your item
- Include any defects or wear

### Location
- Select your city/region
- Add specific location details if needed

## Step 4: Contact Information

- Verify your phone number
- Choose your preferred contact method
- Set availability hours

## Step 5: Review and Publish

1. Review all information for accuracy
2. Check photos and descriptions
3. Click **Publish Ad**
4. Your ad will go live after moderation

## Tips for Success

- Use clear, well-lit photos
- Write honest descriptions
- Price competitively
- Respond quickly to inquiries
- Meet in safe, public places
