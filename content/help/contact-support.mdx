# Contact Support

Need help? We’re here for you. Whether you’re experiencing a technical issue, have billing questions, or simply want guidance on how to use our platform, our support team is ready to assist.

---

## 🛠️ When to Contact Support

You should reach out to support if:

- You’re encountering an error or bug
- You can’t access your account or dashboard
- You have a question about billing or refunds
- You need help using a feature
- You want to report abusive or suspicious behavior

<tip>Before contacting support, check our [Help Center](/help-center) — many common questions are answered there!</tip>

---

## 📬 How to Reach Us

We offer multiple ways to get in touch with our support team:

### 📧 Email Support

For general inquiries or issues that don’t need immediate attention, send us an email:

**support@example.com**  
_Response time: Within 24 hours_

### 💬 Live Chat (Recommended)

Chat with a support agent in real-time directly from our website.

- Available: **Mon–Fri, 9 AM – 6 PM (GMT+6)**
- Just click the chat bubble at the bottom-right corner of any page

### 📞 Phone Support (Premium Users Only)

If you’re on a Premium plan, you can schedule a call with one of our agents. Visit your [account dashboard](/dashboard/settings) to book a time.

---

## 📝 What to Include in Your Message

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

- Your full name and email address
- A detailed description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce the issue (if applicable)
- Screenshots or error messages

<warning>Vague support requests may delay resolution. The more details you give, the faster we can help.</warning>

---

If your request is urgent and not getting attention, reply to your original support email with the word **“URGENT”** in the subject line. This will prioritize your ticket.
